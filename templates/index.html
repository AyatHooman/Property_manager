🚀 Sale & net benefit
Estimated sale price (after n yrs) —
Price × (1 + growth% × years).
− Selling costs —
Capital gain (CGT-exempt) —
Sale price − purchase price. Your main residence is exempt from capital gains tax, so the whole gain is yours.
— real gain (above inflation) —
Gain adjusted for inflation over the holding period — your true purchasing-power gain.
Net benefit on sale —
Capital gain − selling costs − holding costs − acquisition fees. Deposit & principal repayments are excluded (recoverable equity). Capital gain is CGT-free for your home. Also excludes the rent you'd otherwise pay — owning's hidden bonus.
